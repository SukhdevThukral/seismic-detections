About the Project

Our mission is to analyze seismic data from planetary missions, specifically from Apollo and Mars InSight. We aim to identify seismic events and contribute to the understanding of planetary seismology.

Data Sources

We utilize various datasets from the Apollo missions and Mars InSight, including:

  • Apollo seismic records
  • Mars InSight seismic records
  • Other relevant datasets

Data Analysis

We are exploring different methodologies, including traditional signal processing and machine learning techniques, to identify and analyze seismic events.

Join us as we explore the data and discover insights!
